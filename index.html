Foodgram

The Foodgram projectis an online recipe publishing service. It allows you to register and log in, change your password and upload a user avatar, create, edit, and delete recipes, add recipes to your favorites, follow other users, filter recipes by tags, create shopping lists, and calculate ingredients.

YaCut

The YaCut project is a link shortening service. Its purpose is to associate a long user-supplied link with a short one, either provided by the user or by the service. YaCut also allows users to upload multiple files to Yandex Disk at once and provide shortened links for downloading them.
